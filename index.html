FAQ

"What if I don't like what you deliver?" +

You're paying for professional judgment, not a guessing game. We start with a thorough brief that gives us everything we need to build a direction that fits your business. The work will be polished, considered, and appropriate for your industry. It may not be exactly what you would have dreamed up — it might be better.

"Is this just a Canva template with my name on it?" +

No. Everything is designed from scratch, specific to your business. Fixed scope doesn't mean low effort — it means a tight, streamlined process that cuts waste, not quality.

"Why not just use Fiverr?" +

You can. You'll get something fast and cheap, and it'll look fast and cheap. This is for businesses that want to look like they take themselves seriously — without paying agency rates to get there.

"What if my business has a very specific vision?" +

Most SMEs don't have a fully-formed visual direction — and that's fine. That's the point of hiring a designer. You fill out the brief, we do the thinking. If you genuinely have a detailed creative direction you need executed precisely, this probably isn't the right fit and we'd rather tell you that upfront.

"What formats do I get?" +

SVG, PNG, PDF, and color variations (full color, black, reversed). Organized and labeled. Ready for your printer, your web developer, your social media manager, and your future self.

"How long does it take?" +

14 business days from the time your brief is submitted. Everything in the final package is print-ready and digital-ready on delivery. No waiting for a second round of files.
